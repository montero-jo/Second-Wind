Second Wind

150 houses powered
within in an hour

The efficiency of clean wind energy generation has been growning at an exponential rate over the last 30 or so years. As the fight against climate change and its sources continues, we can find an optimistic light in the progression of renewable energies.

The visual on your left represents an increase in the production efficiency of wind turbines since the 1990s. Each one of the squares represents 150 houses' electricity needs being met for around a month. Take in mind that, for the most part, this is generated within an hour or so of function.
